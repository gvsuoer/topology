Item.

\(a_n\) is the function defined by
\begin{equation*} a_n(x) = \frac{1}{n}x \end{equation*}
where \(X\) is the set of real valued functions on the interval \([0,1]\) and the metric \(d\) is defined by
\begin{equation*} d(f,g) = \sup\{|f(x)-g(x)| \mid x \in [0,1]\}\text{.} \end{equation*}
