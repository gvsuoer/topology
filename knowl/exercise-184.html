Describe each of the following quotient spaces.

(a)

\(X\) is the closed interval \([0,1]\) in \(\R\) and \(A = \{0,1\}\)

(b)

\(X = \{(x,y) \mid x^2+y^2 = 1\}\text{,}\) \(A = \{(-1,0), (1,0)\}\)
