\begin{equation*} d(x,y) = \sqrt{\sum_{i=1}^n d_i(x_i,y_i)^2} \end{equation*}
when \(x = (x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_n)\) and \(y = (y_1, y_2, \ldots, y_n)\) are in \(X\text{.}\) Show that \(d\) is a metric on \(\prod_{i=1}^n X_i\text{.}\)
