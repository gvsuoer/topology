Item.

Let \(X = \R\) with the Euclidean metric. Let \(A_n = \left(1-\frac{1}{n}, 1+\frac{1}{n}\right)\) for each \(n \in \Z^+\text{.}\)
  1. What is \(\bigcup_{n \geq 1} A_n\text{?}\) A proof is not necessary.
  2. Is \(\bigcup_{n \geq 1} A_n\) open in \(\R\text{?}\) Explain.
  3. What is \(\bigcap_{n \geq 1} A_n\text{?}\) A proof is not necessary.
  4. Is \(\bigcap_{n \geq 1} A_n\) open in \(\R\text{?}\) Explain.
