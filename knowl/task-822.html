Describe quotient spaces of \(X = I \times I\) with standard topology given by the following equivalence relations \(\sim\text{.}\) Depictions of the identifications are shown in FigureÂ 16.5. (Here \(I\) is the closed interval \([0,1]\text{.}\))
(i)
\((x, y) \sim (x,y)\) if \(0 \lt y \lt 1\) and \(0 \leq x \leq 1\) and \((x,0) \sim (1-x,0)\) when \(0 \leq x \leq 1\text{.}\)
