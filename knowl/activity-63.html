Activity 13.3.

Find the limit point(s) of the following sets

(a)

\(\{c,d\}\) in \((X, \tau)\) with \(X= \{a,b,c,d\}\) and \(\tau = \{\emptyset, \{a\}, \{b\}, \{a,b\}, X \}\)

(b)

\(\{a,b\}\) in the set \(X= \{a,b,c,d,e,f\}\) with topology
\begin{equation*} \tau= \{\emptyset,\{b\}, \{a,b,c\},\{d,e,f\},\{b,d,e,f\}, X\}\text{.} \end{equation*}

(c)

\(\{a,b\} \subset X\) where \(X = \{a,b,c\}\) in the discrete topology.

(d)

\(\{-1,0,1\} \subset \Z\) with \(\tau\) the topology on \(\Z\) with basis \(\{B(n)\}\text{,}\) where
\begin{equation*} B(n) = \begin{cases}\{n\} \amp \text{ if \(n\) is odd } , \\ \{n-1,n,n+1\} \amp \text{ if \(n\) is even } . \end{cases} \end{equation*}
(This topology is called the digital line topology and has applications in digital processing. That the collection \(\{B(n)\}\) is a basis for a topology on \(\Z\) is the topic of ExerciseÂ 11)
