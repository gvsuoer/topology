Definition 17.3.

A subcover of a cover \(\{S_{\alpha}\}_{\alpha \in I}\) of a subset \(A\) of a topological space \(X\) is a collection \(\{S_{\beta}\}\) for \(\beta \in J\text{,}\) where \(J\) is a subset of \(I\) such that \(A \subseteq \bigcup_{\beta \in J} S_{\beta}\text{.}\) In addition, if \(J\) is a finite set, the subcover \(\{S_{\beta}\}_{\beta \in J}\) is a finite subcover of \(\{S_{\alpha}\}_{\alpha \in I}\text{.}\)
