A subset \(A\) of a topological space \(X\) is compact if for every set \(I\) and every family of open sets \(\{O_{\alpha}\}\) with \(\alpha \in I\) such that \(A \subseteq \bigcup_{\alpha \in I} O_{\alpha}\text{,}\) there exists a finite subfamily \(\{O_{\alpha_1}, O_{\alpha_2}, \ldots, O_{\alpha_n}\}\) such that \(A \subseteq \bigcup_{i = 1}^n O_{\alpha_i}\text{.}\)
