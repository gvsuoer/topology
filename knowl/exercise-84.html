Exercise 5.

Let \(A = \{(x,y) \in \R^2 \mid 1 \lt x \lt 3, 0 \lt y \lt 1\}\text{.}\)

(a)

Is \(A\) an open set in \((\R^2, d_E)\text{?}\) Prove your answer.

(b)

Is \(A\) an open set in \((\R^2, d_T)\text{?}\) Prove your answer.

(c)

Is \(A\) an open set in \((\R^2, d_M)\text{?}\) Prove your answer.
