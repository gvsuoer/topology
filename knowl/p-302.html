Let \(f : X \to Y\) be a function and let \(\{A_{\alpha}\}\) be a collection of subsets of \(X\) for \(\alpha\) in some indexing set \(I\text{,}\) and \(\{B_{\beta}\}\) be a collection of subsets of \(Y\) for \(\beta\) in some indexing set \(J\text{.}\) Then
  1. \(f\left(\bigcup_{\alpha \in I} A_{\alpha}\right) = \bigcup_{\alpha \in I} f(A_{\alpha})\) and
  2. \(f^{-1}\left(\bigcup_{\beta \in J} B_{\beta}\right) = \bigcup_{\beta \in J} f^{-1}(B_{\beta})\text{.}\)
