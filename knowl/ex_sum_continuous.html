Exercise 5.

Let \(f\) and \(g\) be continuous functions from \((\R,d_E)\) to \((\R, d_E)\text{.}\)

(a)

Let \(k \in \R\) with \(k \neq 0\) and define \(kf : \R \to \R\) by \((kf)(x) = kf(x)\) for all \(x \in \R\text{.}\) Show that \(kf\) is a continuous function.

(b)

Define \(f+g : \R \to \R\) by \((f+g)(x) = f(x) + g(x)\) for all \(x \in \R\text{.}\) Show that \(f+g\) is a continuous function.
