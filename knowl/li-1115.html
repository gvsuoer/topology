Definition 15.1.3.

Let \(f: X \to Y\) be a function from a topological space \(X\) to a topological space \(Y\text{.}\) Then \(f\) is an open function if \(f(U)\) is open in \(Y\) whenever \(U\) is open in \(X\text{.}\)
