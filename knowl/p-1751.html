Let \(X\) be a set on which two metrics \(d\) and \(d'\) are defined. If there exist positive constants \(K\) and \(K'\) so that
\begin{align*} d'(x,y) \amp \leq K d(x,y)\\ d(x,y) \amp \leq K' d'(x,y) \end{align*}
for all \(x,y \in X\text{,}\) then \((X,d)\) is topologically equivalent to \((X,d')\text{.}\)
