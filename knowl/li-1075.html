Item 12.

For each integer \(a\text{,}\) let \(a\Z = \{ka \mid k \in \Z\}\text{.}\) That is, \(a\Z\) is the set of all integer multiples of \(a\text{.}\) That \(\{a\Z \mid a \in \Z\}\) is a basis for a topology \(\tau\) on \(\Z\) is the topic of Exercise (ex_aZ_top) on [cross-reference to target(s) "ex_aZ_top" missing or not unique]. In this exercise work in the topological space \((\Z, \tau)\)
  • Let \(A = \mathbb{E}\text{,}\) the set of even integers.
    1. Find, with justification, \(\Int(A)\text{.}\)
    2. Find, with justification, \(\overline{A}\text{.}\)
  • Let \(B = \mathbb{N} = \{n \in \Z \mid n \geq 1\}\text{.}\) That is, \(\mathbb{N}\) is the set of natural numbers.
    1. Find, with justification, \(\Int(B)\text{.}\)
    2. Find, with justification, \(\overline{B}\text{.}\)
