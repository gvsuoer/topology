\begin{equation*} f(x) = \tan\left(\pi\left(x-\frac{1}{2}\right)\right)\text{.} \end{equation*}
  1. Explain why \(f\) maps \((0,1)\) to \(\R\text{.}\)
  2. Explain why \(f\) is an injection.
  3. Explain why \(f\) is a surjection.
  4. Explain why \(f\) and \(f^{-1}\) are continuous. Hint.
