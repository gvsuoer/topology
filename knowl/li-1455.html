Item.

Suppose that \(U\) and \(V\) are disjoint nonempty open sets in \(Z\) such that \(U \cup V = Z\text{.}\) Let \(R = g^{-1}(U)\) and \(S = g^{-1}(V)\text{.}\)
  1. Explain why \(R\) and \(S\) are open sets in \(X\text{.}\)
  2. Show that \(R \cup S = X\text{.}\) (Hint: \(X = g^{-1}(Z)\text{.}\))
  3. Show \(R\) and \(S\) are nonempty sets. Hint.
  4. Now show that \(R \cap S = \emptyset\text{.}\) (Hint: \(R \cap S = g^{-1}(U) \cap g^{-1}(V)\text{.}\))
