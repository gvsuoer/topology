Let \(X\) be the subspace of \((\R^2, d_E)\) consisting of the line segments joining the point \((0,1)\) to every point in the set \(\left\{\left(\frac{1}{n}, 0\right) \mid n \in \Z^+\right\}\) as illustrated in FigureÂ 19.19. This space is called the harmonic broom.

(a)

Show that the harmonic broom is connected.

(b)

(c)
