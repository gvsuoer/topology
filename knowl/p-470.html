Let \(n\) be a positive integer and \(x = (x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_n)\text{,}\) \(y=(y_1, y_2, \ldots, y_n)\) be in \(\R^n\text{.}\) To verify (3.1) it suffices to show that
\begin{equation*} \left(\sum_{i=1}^n x_iy_i\right)^2 \leq \left(\sum_{i=1}^n x_i^2\right) \left(\sum_{i=1}^n y_i^2\right)\text{.} \end{equation*}
