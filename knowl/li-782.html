Item 2.

Let \((X,d)\) be a metric space. Let \(a \in X\text{,}\) and let \(r > 0\text{.}\) We know that the open ball \(B(a,r) = \{x \in X \mid d(a,x) \lt r\}\) is an open set. Let
\begin{equation*} B[a,r] = \{x \in X \mid d(a,x) \leq r\}\text{.} \end{equation*}
Prove or disprove: \(B[a,r]\) is a closed set in \(X\text{.}\)
