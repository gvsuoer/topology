Let \(X\) be a topological space, and let \(A_{\alpha}\) be a connected subset of \(X\) for all \(\alpha\) in some indexing set \(I\text{.}\) Let \(B\) be a connected subset of \(X\) such that \(A_{\alpha} \cap B \neq \emptyset\) for every \(\alpha \in I\text{.}\) Then \(B \cup \left(\bigcup_{\alpha \in I} A_{\alpha} \right)\) is connected.
