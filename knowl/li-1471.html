Complete the proof of the following lemma.

Lemma 19.3.1.

Let \(X\) be a topological space, and let \(A\) be a subset of \(X\text{.}\) Assume that \(U\) and \(V\) form a separation of \(A\text{.}\) If \(C\) is a connected subset of \(A\text{,}\) then \(C \subseteq U\) or \(C \subseteq V\text{.}\)
