Item.

Let \(\left\{U_{\beta}\right\}\) be a collection of sets in \(\tau_Y\) for \(\beta\) in some indexing set \(J\text{.}\)
  1. Show that \(\bigcup_{\beta \in J} U_{\beta}\) is in \(\tau_Y\text{.}\)
  2. If \(J\) is finite, show that \(\bigcap_{\beta \in J} U_{\beta}\) is in \(\tau_Y\text{.}\)
