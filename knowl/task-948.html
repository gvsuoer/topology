(i)
Explain why \(R\) and \(S\) are open sets in \(X\text{.}\)
(ii)
Show that \(R \cup S = X\text{.}\) (Hint: \(X = g^{-1}(Z)\text{.}\))
(iii)
Show \(R\) and \(S\) are nonempty sets.
Hint.
(iv)
Now show that \(R \cap S = \emptyset\text{.}\) (Hint: \(R \cap S = g^{-1}(U) \cap g^{-1}(V)\text{.}\))
